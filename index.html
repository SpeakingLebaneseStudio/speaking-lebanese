How it works

  1. Translate tab — fill in French, Lebanese transliteration, Arabic, and gender/tense forms. Auto-saves every keystroke.
  2. Record tab — record audio one word at a time. Each recording is saved immediately.
    3. 📚 A1/A2 Curriculum Words

    Checks the database for missing A1 and A2 curriculum words and adds any that are absent.

  3. Browse tab — search and filter the whole vocabulary, edit any word, play any recording.

If a save fails (e.g. no internet), it's queued locally and retried automatically when the connection returns. You will never lose work.
