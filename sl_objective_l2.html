Numbers & Personal Info

By the end of this lesson, you'll be able to give your age, your phone number, your job, and your nationality — the way a Lebanese person actually says them, not the way you'd translate them in your head.

Your age. Your number. Your name from Lebanon. Three things everyone will ask you — and by tonight, you'll answer all three without pausing to translate.
