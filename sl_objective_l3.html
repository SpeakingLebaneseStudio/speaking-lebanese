Lesson Objective
Family & Relationships

By the end of this lesson, you'll be able to introduce your family, ask questions about someone else's, say who belongs to whom, and know who's married — the way a Lebanese person actually says it, not the way you'd translate it in your head.

Your family. Your questions. Your place at the table. Once you can say whose is whose, you stop being the guest — you become part of it.
