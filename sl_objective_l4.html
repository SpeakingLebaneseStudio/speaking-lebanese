Lesson Objective
Food & Drink / Ordering

By the end of this lesson, you'll be able to order confidently at a Lebanese table — ask what's available, say what you want, point to this dish or that one, and describe what's in front of you — the way Lebanese people actually talk, not the way you'd translate it in your head.

Baddi hayda bass, law sama7t! Three words, and the whole table already knows what you're having.
