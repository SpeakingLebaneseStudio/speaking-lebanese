8

The 8 sound

غ

A soft, voiced gargle from the back of the throat — like the French "r" in "Paris," or gargling water while humming. It's made in the same place as the 5 (خ) sound from Lesson 2, but voiced: your throat should buzz slightly, where 5 is just breath.

Shadow Prompt — Sound 8
Z8eeret w 8aalyet 3layyi.

Young and precious to me. Listen. Repeat during the silence. Listen again and compare. You will hear the phrase twice with a 3-second gap.

The 8 sound is just a voiced version of the 5 you already learned last lesson — same spot in the throat, just add your voice. Say "5," then try to hum through it. That hum is exactly "8."
