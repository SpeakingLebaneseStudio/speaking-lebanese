9

The 9 sound

ص

An emphatic, "heavier" S. Round your lips slightly, pull the back of your tongue down and back in your mouth, and push air through for a hissing S — like the s in "saw," but deeper and rounder, as if the sound has more weight behind it. Regular English S stays light and forward; this one drags backward.

Shadow Prompt — Sound 9
Baddi 3a9eer a9far.

I want yellow juice. Listen. Repeat during the silence. Listen again and compare. You will hear the phrase twice with a 3-second gap.

ص is the last of the seven Lebanese sounds in this course — once this one feels natural, every sound in the dialect is yours. Round your lips, pull your tongue back, and let it feel heavier than the English S.
