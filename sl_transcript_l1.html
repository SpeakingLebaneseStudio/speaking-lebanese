Setting: A voicemail from Tante Mona, calling from Jounieh. She hasn't heard from you in a while.

01
Allo? Allo!
Hello? Hello!
02
2ana Tante Mona! 3am be7keek min Jounieh, eh eh, 2ana hiyyi Mona, Mona Khoury!
I'm Aunt Mona! I'm calling you from Jounieh, yes yes, it's me — Mona, Mona Khoury!
3am be7keek — I'm calling you
03
Keefak 2enta? Nchallah mnee7? El 7amdellah 2ana mnee7a, merci!
How are you? Are you well? Thank God I'm fine, thank you!
El 7amdellah — Thank God (standard response to "how are you")
04
Zamén ma smi3na sawtak! Walla zamén! Killna hown, killna 3am nes2al 3annak!
It's been so long since we heard your voice! Really so long! We're all here, everyone's asking about you!
Walla — really / I swear (emphatic)
05
Yalla, 2ahlan wa sahlan feek deyman — beitna houwwi beitak! Ta3a la 3inna! Walla, kteer min7ebb nchoufak, 3an jadd, kteer!
Come on, you're always welcome — our home is your home! Come over, we'd love to see you so much, really!
2ahlan wa sahlan — you are most welcome
06
Min fadlak bass feek, 2ittossil fiyyi — 2ana natritak, ok? Yalla!
Please, just call me back — I'm waiting for you!
2ittossil — call (imperative) / natritak — I'm waiting for you
07
Yalla bye 7abibi, 3afwan 2iza 2az3ajtak. Ma3 el salémet! Shoukran!
Okay bye habibi, sorry if I bothered you. Take care! Thank you!
7abibi — my love / darling (term of endearment)
08
Ahhh w kamén, sallim, sallim kteer 3al ahl killon!
And also, give my warmest regards to all the family!
Sallim 3al ahl — give my regards to the family
